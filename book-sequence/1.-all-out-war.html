An unpredictable event, caught between truth and shadow. The two empires and a kingdom of beasts stands at the brink of war, but can power holders keep the balance and save the world just like a millennium ago or join the war for salvation. While the history needs the justice the most, can a frail prophetic child rise from darkness and put an end to the upcoming chaos?

Three centuries ago world peace took place until the two races controlled by worldly desires and revenge emerged to take over with a full scale war. Spoiled Summary

The mainCh sees a dream about the outer universe, galaxies and suddenly everything changes into the smallest particle and he suddenly wakes up in the middle of the night. Something awakens in him but he does not realize anything and he does not know what he saw exactly because of his knowledge level and he goes to sleep after a sip of water. Next morning he hears some guards are talking about an award winning championship on the warrior arena which he wants to see so much because since he was born he could not be able to leave the royal palace without any permissions from the empress. He wants to discover what is outside of the palace. After a week later at the day of the arena championship, when the number of guards are low he escapes from the palace to visit the arena. Over there he gets to know each other with the Magic master and becomes friends. Later they together joins the royal army of the south. A huge news comes from the North empire about a never ending fight between two power holders called Strength body and Strength master. The North E. requests troops to stop the fight from South and when the conflict is solved Strength body joins the dreamer's team but Strength master vanishes without a trace.
338|After a short amount time later they hear about a New Beast War is about to start soon. The North and South comes to an agreement in the great military summit/console (with the kind of help of the solved conflict) and they select a Supreme commander from North E. to represent both armies of South and North. All generals falls under the Supreme commander's command for the war preparations. While these are happening the dreamer's team discovers the corrupt government system under the North E. and some clues from the sacred library of Northern palace the clues leads them to a graveyard which is supposedly needs to be the great emperor's grave but instead they find an already opened tomb stone leading them to a underground staircase. When they reach the main corridor they find someone really old over ground laying with a girl beside her which they later find out that she was the nightmare. but when she realizes them she escapes with some tricks. After they help the great emperor heal and learn the truth about the old peaceful land and the ancient beast war details. They immediately try to go and stop the war however the nightmare left them a surprise and they somehow unable to leave because of the illusory tunnels and they get stuck there for almost a month. When they solve the puzzles of the nightmare and finally escape from the underground, they find themselves in an unbelievable situation which the new beast war has already started and the beast kingdom is almost has lost to the Supreme commander. However at that further into the war area on the side of Beast K. they see a summoning platform with 13 people on it and while the dreamer's team tries to stop the summon, the war ground turns upside down with a mythical dragon with the height of a mountain which can destroy the whole city and royal palace with one hand. After the summon the supreme commender surrenders to the Beast Emperor and gets to be the right hand man of him. The dreamer's team hardly escapes could not be able to do anything and with countless wounds on them. The Two Empire's gets doomed as a result and the Beast Kingdom armies and solders slowly captures the all land left by the Empires and dominates the world. While A Kill Bounty list has issued for the capture for dreamer's team. The nightmare as the lead and her group of assassins tries every way annihilate the dreamer and his team. The barriers of the capital cities are almost broken and when the mythical dragon arrives, the castle walls falls and nothing stays still from the flames and a nightmare will be upon the people of the Empires. Will dreamer accomplish the world peace by awaking his abilities and use moon's power to replenish the peace upon the world?

Goal of Empires: Defeat the Beast king, to regain world peace and order. To full fill the request from the great emperor's scroll. However corrupt government system has its roots in the Northern Empire which leads to unexpected twists.

All Empire and Kingdoms are trying to use the power holders to their advantage in the war and further in the book, it will cause to downfall of the world with the defeat of dreamer and his allies.

The deceptions, lies and illusions will twist your understanding of the book, in the later chapters and also in the next book we will try to explain what was really happening, so do not believe everything you see or hear in this book about the story's details.
