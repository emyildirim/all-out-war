Kingdom Of Peace

A peaceful land was living in the dark without any wars or lack of love but one summer night everything was about to change, the two nations had born without any warning and the first couple of decades was usual as peaceful as ever then before but an unexpected event occurred in an unknown street which created the first scars of its kind to its nation. A butterfly effect later, this scar of nation turned into a war that will be called Ancient Beast War, however the leaders of the nations, the great emperor tried to settle the war between these two races but when the news of the war reached to the empire, it was already too late and it was too unusual that no body wanted to believe such a thing in a land that has forgotten what "war" is because that was how peaceful the empire was. When the swords and spears were taken to war the Humans and Beasts could not bear to see each other so when the empire’s royal soldiers came to put an end to it, they destroyed the soldiers without any hesitation, the war continued every day and night because peace was never an option for them. The great emperor could not stop any one of the sides . At the end of the Ancient Beast War as a result, the empire divided into three continents containing: Beast Kingdom, Northern Empire, Southern Empire. Between the Empires and the Beast Kingdom, the unhabitable area called Wastelands created which made the border for the countries.

Also based on a legend, the only anomalies wasn’t this war, some strange power holders rose through the world. The fighting arenas went popular during the after war era and this time, the previous peace has replaced by pleasure and desires of the living.

According to a rumor, Initially there was 12 kings and 1 emperor and they have all agreed and voted for the project with details not know to man. The project worked for almost 800 years and kept the world in peace but one day, someone from the darkness (legend of chaos) warned the people of the beast by showing an exit from the project which caused a disturbtion. Caused a war between humans and beasts later called "ancient beast war". Beasts did not wanted to believe any words of humans anymore. This revolution brought a new idea to beasts about peace and, what they learned from old kingdom of peace was that it was never a equal peace. However based on some sources of human nations, that 12 kings who decided the fate of the world, there were 4 beast kings among them. It is said that one of the 13 has betrayed the empire and became the one called "devil of chaos". However no one has seen this person, it was only a legend that tied the Kingdom of Peace and the current political placeholders.

Southern Empire and Northern Empire were united in the past, but because of some small grudges between blood relatives of Great Emperor, The heir family and the relatives separated in their own ways and they do not attack each other to show respect to the Great Emperor. However South holds much more military and financial power than North and this made North even a greater target for outsiders. Later the country became corrupted from inside with fake and illusiory government officials under the Northern Kings. Even the generals and some Kings of the North became a slave and a servant to the Beast King.
